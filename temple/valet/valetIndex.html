  • Good news: The Temple has scheduled a half-day meditation workshop for Saturday, August 20, 2016 between 1Pm and 5pm. You can see the detailed schedule by clicking the Meditation link below and selecting Meditation Workshop 08-20-2016.
  • First time visitors: The Sunday Market is cash only. The closest ATM is located at the 7-11 (about 1/3 mile west of the Temple) on Palm River Road.

Meditation

Meditation is a conscious effort to change how the mind works. The Pali word for meditation is 'bhavana' which means 'to make grow' or 'to develop'.

Wat Mongkolratanaram

Wat Mongkolratanaram is a Buddhist Thai temple on the bank of the Palm River in Tampa, Florida. It is known for its Sunday food market.
